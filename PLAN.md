# Tab Bar Cleanup + Community Tab Polish

## Part 9: Tab Bar Cleanup

**Changes to the main tab bar:**
- Switch all tab icons to outline variants (house, wallet.bifold, wrench.and.screwdriver, bubble.left.and.bubble.right, person.circle) — no more filled icons
- Active tab shows in emerald accent color, inactive tabs in muted gray
- Remove the floating green "+" button from the Community tab

## Part 10: Community Tab Polish

**Mood tags redesign:**
- All mood tags (Hopeful, Struggling, Grateful, etc.) use a single neutral style — gray text on a dark elevated pill, no more colored indicators

**Avatar redesign:**
- Post avatars get a glass-style circular frame with a subtle emerald border instead of mood-colored backgrounds

**Category tags:**
- All category pills (Victories, Struggles, Tips, Questions) also use a single muted style — no more per-category colors

**Like button:**
- Liked state uses emerald accent instead of red

**New "Share your story" card:**
- An integrated input-style card at the top of the feed replaces the floating button: "Share your story..." with a pencil icon, tappable to open the create post sheet

**Partner section:**
- All teal-colored elements in the partner and challenges sections switch to emerald accent for consistency
