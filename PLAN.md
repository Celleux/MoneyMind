# Restructure Tools Tab with Categories & Toned-Down Emergency

## What's Changing

The Tools screen will be completely reorganized from a flat grid of clinical-sounding tools into **3 clear categories** with plain-language labels, plus a subtle crisis support link at the bottom.

---

### **Features**

- Tools are grouped into **3 sections**: Spending Tools, Impulse Control, and Coaching & Therapy
- Each section has a clear header with an icon
- All tool names use **plain, friendly language** instead of clinical terms
- Every tool card shows a short description of what it does
- A subtle "Need urgent help?" link sits at the bottom — accessible but not alarming
- The red "Having an urge right now?" banner at the top is **removed**
- The pulsing emergency animation is **removed**
- The floating red SOS button on the main tab bar is **removed** (crisis support is still 2 taps away via the subtle link in Tools)
- The floating purple "+" FAB button on the tab bar is **removed** (the "Add" action already lives in the Home quick actions)

---

### **Design**

- **Spending Tools** section (2×2 grid):
  - Budget Tracker — "Track your spending across categories"
  - Ghost Budget — "What-if scenarios for your money"
  - Vibe Check — "How do you feel about that purchase?"
  - Challenges — "Savings goals and streaks"

- **Impulse Control** section (2×2 grid):
  - Pause & Breathe — "Guided breathing before buying"
  - Cool Down — "Wait it out with a countdown timer"
  - Urge Journal — "Log what triggered the urge"
  - If-Then Plan — "Pre-set your response to triggers"

- **Coaching & Therapy** section (2×1 grid + full-width coach card):
  - AI Money Coach — full-width card: "Talk through what you're feeling"
  - Exercises — "CBT & ACT techniques"

- **Crisis Support** — small, muted text at the very bottom: "Need urgent help? Tap for immediate support →"

- All tool card icons use **one color (emerald accent)** — no more rainbow of different colors per card
- All cards use the existing glass card style with subtle depth
- Section headers are clean with an emerald-tinted icon
- The Blocking & Friction section (DNS blocking, breathing pause) stays but moves under Impulse Control as additional items

---

### **Screen: Tools Tab**

- Scrollable screen with a "Tools" navigation title
- Subtitle under the title: "What do you need right now?"
- Three collapsible-style sections, each with a 2×2 grid of glass tool cards
- Full-width AI Coach card between the grid sections
- Subtle crisis link at the very bottom in muted text
- Tapping any tool card opens the same full-screen view it does today (no navigation changes)
- The DNS blocking and breathing pause cards appear as additional rows under Impulse Control

---

### **Tab Bar Changes**

- The floating red SOS button and floating green "+" FAB button are both removed from the main tab bar overlay
- Tab bar stays clean: Home, Wallet, Tools, Community, Profile
